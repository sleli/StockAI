Why this works

An editorial terminal for everyday investors.

The memorable gesture is the contrast between warm financial-journal typography and sharp exchange-board data modules. It feels trustworthy, quieter than trading software, and more intentional than an ad-heavy finance portal.

User scenario
"I open it before the bell, see what changed, and understand whether the number is fresh enough to trust."

Marco checks from his phone during the commute. Giulia opens the same dashboard later on a laptop to compare movements and study a stock's short history. Both need confidence, not complexity.

MVP represented
Watchlist, search, current price, change, freshness states, short history, and alert thresholds.
Constraint respected
The interface assumes near-real-time free APIs and keeps timestamps visible to manage expectations.
Design direction
Financial broadsheet meets calm terminal: warm paper, ink, deep green, and red reserved for risk.
